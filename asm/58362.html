E3FA: flip_24_masked_pixels
Takes the 24 pixels in E,C,B and reverses them bitwise.
Does the same for the mask pixels in E',C',B'.
Roll the bitmap.
E First 8 pixels.
BC Second 16 pixels.
E Reversed pixels.
BC Reversed pixels.
