E40F: flip_16_masked_pixels
Used by the routines at E2A2 and E34E.
Takes the 16 pixels in D,E and reverses them bitwise.
Does the same for the mask pixels in D',E'.
Roll the bitmap.
Input
DE 16 pixels to reverse.
Output
DE Reversed pixels.
